Route 401: Getting Ready for Real Analysis

Sarah Milligan
Bridgewater State University
January 10, 2021

Are your skills ready for real analysis? These materials form a short review course to help you brush up on important prerequisite skills from your past courses in calculus and proofs. These skills can make or break your experience with real analysis, so we hope this resource will be helpful as you get ready to tackle the course in the upcoming semester!

Use the section navigation links at left to access review materials, videos, and workbook problems.

Join Route 401 on Discord (you'll receive an invitation link in your email) to chat and interact with your fellow classmates around these materials.

Download this workbook as a PDF using this link: route401-s21.pdf.

These materials were assembled and sponsored by the Bridgewater State University Math Club, adapting materials shared by BSU math faculty members.
