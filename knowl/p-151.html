A subset \(I\subseteq\mathbb{R}\) is called an interval if, for all \(x,y,z\in\mathbb{R}\) with \(x \leq y \leq z\text{,}\) we have

\begin{equation*} \text{If }x,z\in I \text{ then }y\in I. \end{equation*}
