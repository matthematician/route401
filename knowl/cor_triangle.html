Corollary 3.9. Triangle Inequalities.

Let \(a,b\in\mathbb{R}\) be real numbers. Then

\begin{align} |a+b| &\leq |a|+|b| && \textsf{(Triangle inequality)}\tag{3.8}\\ |a-b| &\geq \biggl| \,|a| - |b|\, \biggr| && \textsf{(Reverse triangle inequality)}\tag{3.9}\\ |a|-|b| \leq |a+b| &\leq |a|+|b|\tag{3.10} \end{align}

Furthermore, for any natural number \(n\geq 2\) and real numbers \(a_1,a_2,a_3,\ldots,a_n\in\mathbb{R}\text{,}\) we have

\begin{equation*} \bigl|a_1+a_2+a_3+\cdots+a_n\bigr| \leq |a_1|+|a_2|+|a_3|+\cdots+|a_n|. \end{equation*}
