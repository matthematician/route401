Use an element argument and establish both subset inclusions. This means proving both:

\begin{align*} \forall x\in A\cup(B\cap C), & \text{ we have } x\in (A\cup B)\cap (A\cup C) &\text{ and }\\ \forall x\in (A\cup B)\cap (A\cup C), & \text{ we have } x\in A\cup (B\cap C). \end{align*}
