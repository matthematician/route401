Worksheet Exercise 7.

Let \(S\) be the universe where \(A,B,C\subseteq S\text{.}\) Let

\begin{align*} S &= \{1,2,3,4,5,6,7\}\\ A &= \{1,2,4\}\\ B &= \{1,3,5,7\}\\ C &= \{2,4,6,7\} \end{align*}

Find each of the following. Drawing a Venn diagram will help!

  1. \(\displaystyle A\cup B\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (A\cup B)\cap C\)

  3. \(\displaystyle C^C \cup B\)

  4. \(\displaystyle (A\cap B)^C\)

  5. \(\displaystyle C\setminus B\)
